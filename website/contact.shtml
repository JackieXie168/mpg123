Contact / Impressum

This site is authored by Michael Hipp and Thomas Orgis, with various content originally written by Michael; Thomas being responsible for the current operation and presentation of the site as well as adding new content.

If you want to get in touch with the mpg123 project, the best way is to head to the sourceforge project site and subscribe to mailing lists, report bugs / wishes, etc.

Some of the developers are also on the #mpg123 IRC channel on the FreeNode IRC network.

Any questions regarding the website should be sent per eMail to the official mpg123 maintainer, Thomas Orgis.
